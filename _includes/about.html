Hi! I am Zhiyuan "Ryan" Wang, currently a Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs Research, working on Behavioral AI and Proactive Agents for next-generation personal wearables.

I received my Ph.D. in Systems and Information Engineering from the University of Virginia, advised by Prof. Laura E. Barnes in the Sensing Systems for Health Lab and Link Lab. My research develops ubiquitous sensing and intervention systems to enhance human health and well-being, through interdisciplinary collaborations spanning medicine, nursing, psychology, and bioethics. I also build AI agent infrastructure—systems that make AI agents work reliably at scale.

Previously, I was a Research Scientist Intern at Meta Reality Labs Research (2025) and an AI/ML Data Scientist Intern at Johnson & Johnson Innovative Medicine (2024). I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Xiamen University, China (2021).

Research Keywords: Digital Health, Ubiquitous Computing, Mobile Computing, Human-Centered AI, Behavioral AI, AI Agent Systems
